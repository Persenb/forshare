Topic 1

EN: If someone asks you to recommend a good high school abroad, which one would it be?
CN: 如果有人让你推荐一所国外好的高中，你会推荐哪所？

Answer: I would recommend Eton College, a famous boarding high school in the United Kingdom. It has a history of over 580 years and has educated many well-known figures, including several British prime ministers and members of the royal family. The school offers excellent academic programs and a wide range of extracurricular activities. What impresses me most is its focus on developing leadership and character, not just academic results. So if someone asks me for advice, Eton College would be my top recommendation.

回答： 我会推荐伊顿公学，这是英国一所著名的寄宿高中。它有超过580年的历史，培养了许多著名人物，包括几位英国首相和皇室成员。学校提供优秀的学术课程和广泛的课外活动。最让我印象深刻的是它注重培养领导力和品格，而不仅仅是学业成绩。所以如果有人问我建议，伊顿公国会是我的首选推荐。
